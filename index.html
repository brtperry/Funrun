Sunday May 13 Blackpool 10K



BLACKPOOL 10K FUN RUN

Thousands of runners took part in the 30th Beaverbrooks Blackpool 10K Fun Run on Sunday 13th May.

Date: Sunday 13th May 2012
Distance: 10km
Start Time: 11:00am
Location: Blackpool Promenade, at the Hilton Hotel

Photographs

Steve McKelvey from Max Images will be taking photographs at the event for us. These will be available for you to buy 2-3 days after the Fun Run from their website www.max-images.com with a % from each sale going to our chosen charity for 2012, the NSPCC.

  • The atmosphere on the promenade was fantastic with serious runners and fun runners all taking part to raise money for charity. The chosen charity for 2012 is the NSPCC supporting their 'Turning the Tide for Blackpool' Appeal and we hope that together we will raise thousands of pounds to help them improve the lives of children across Blackpool.

    We thank all our runners and the crowds who lined the route, we hope you all had a great time!

    We look forward to seeing you all again next year.

Celebrating 30 Years

The Beaverbrooks Blackpool 10K Fun Run first started in 1982 and has grown in popularity ever since. Attracting thousands of runners each year, the event is organised by the St Annes Coordination of Charities along with Beaverbrooks the Jewellers, The Blackpool Gazette, Radio Wave and the Hilton Hotel, all of whom have provided overwhelming and fundamental support over the last 30 years. The event also wouldn't be possible without the help of various other individuals and organisations including Blackpool Tourism Department, the local Police force, and the 200 marshalls that give up their time on a voluntary basis.

Each year the chosen charity changes and we're extremely proud to have raised over £1.5 million for charities including Cancer Research UK, Manchester Royal Children's Hospital, Trinity Hospice, Brian House Children's Hospice and Blackpool Victoria Hospital for the Blue Skies & Prostate Cancer Appeals. This money has helped to save lives both in the local area and beyond. In addition to these chosen charities, the Fun Run is also a great way for local charities to raise money; organisers estimate that this additional fundraising takes the total figure to over £3 million. We couldn't have done this without your support and so would like to say a massive THANK YOU. Please help us to continue raising money and saving lives by taking part, donating or fundraising. Together let's make 2012 the biggest year yet and celebrate 30 years in style.

From all of us here at the charity, thanks in advance for your support.
